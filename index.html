The Benefits of IT Staff Augmentation

Staff augmentation is a cooperative model where the in-house team hires a specialist from outside for a time being. In the IT staff augmentation process, a skilled developer is resourced from a vendor.

How is Staff Augmentation different from Project Outsourcing?

IT Staff Augmentation

Developer is being hired on a monthly basis or hourly basis from the software outsourcing company

Project Outsourcing

Complete project is being outsourced to software outsourcing company

What are the benefits of Staff Augmentation?

Saves Time

When you opt for staff augmentation to fill in a specialist role in your project, you will have no need to hire a recruiter or go through the tedious process of recruiting.

More Flexible than a Traditional Recruitment

When a company opts for staff augmentation, it can work with any number of people that they want to.

Enhanced Communication

When you rope in IT staff augmentation services you can be in direct contact with them. The project owner can assign tasks, supervise their work.

Increased Productivity

With resource and staff augmentation, the developers have the right skill-set and are solely focused on the job that you have assigned which causes a surge in productivity.

Cost-Effective

If hiring someone full-time is not the exact need of the hour then IT staff augmentation is the best way out for you. The augmentation rates are affordable than hiring full-time employees.

How does the hiring process work in Staff Augmentation ?

If the project can be completed by the in-house team you will not have to hire a specialist with IT staff augmentation.

Once you are clear about the requirements, a job description is structured which suits all the stakeholders.

Based on the job description and the role the candidate will be asked to fulfill, applications are doled out.

At the final stage, the application of the candidate will be interviewed.

The application will be evaluated according to certain parameters set by the company that has opted for resource and staff augmentation. The factors that are taken into consideration are:

English proficiency

Overall experience in the industry

Niche experience pertaining to the role that is being recruited for

Comparison of experience that the candidate has acquired against the desired experience

Locational advantage

The time period within which the developer will be available

Cost of hiring

Questions that a recruiter needs to ask before they resource augmentation services

Question the validation process of the IT staff augmentation process

Vetting the procedure with which large IT staff augmentation firms should be the first priority. Most IT staff augmentation firms look for embellished resumes which is why their technical skills might not be tested in the process.

Enquire about their contributions to specific projects in the past

If you are hiring an IT staff augmentation team from a large company be sure to read up on their past experiences as members of a project. It is very easy to slip in an under-qualified or inexperienced developer into a team.

Define the relationship with the developer better

The best advice one can give to the recruiter is probably the advice of interviewing the IT team augmentation member as one would interview an internal developer. A work culture can benefit from the intersectionality of technical and soft skills.
